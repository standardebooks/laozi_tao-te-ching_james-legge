The Tao Te Ching
was written around the sixth century BC by
Laozi.
It was translated from Classical Chinese in 1891 by
James Legge.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2008 by
Gregory Walker and David Widger
for
Project Gutenberg.

The cover page is adapted from
The Spring Evening Banquet at the Peach and Pear Blossom Garden,
a painting completed between 1677 and 1742 by
Leng Mei.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
